Mancala Rules:

  • The numbers represent the number of stones in each pot
  • To deslect the pot you chose, click it again
  • To make your move, press the "Pick Pot" button
  • Always pick a pot in the top row
  • You cannot pick a pot with 0 stones in it
  • If you land on your own side in an empty pot, you capture that stone and all sones in the pot accross from it
  • If you land in your pot you get to go agai
  • The game ends when one side is completely empty or one pot has more than 25 stones
  • When one side is empty, the other side's pot captures all remaining stones
